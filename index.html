Introduction

Meal Meter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, height, and weight that you provide during account setup.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Calorie intake, meal logs, water consumption, and nutritional information.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.
  • Photos: Only when you choose to analyze food using the camera feature. Photos are processed but not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate personalized calorie goals and nutritional recommendations.
  • Track your daily food intake and progress toward your health goals.
  • Provide AI-powered food analysis features.
  • Improve our app and user experience.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All your personal data, meal logs, and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.
  • Cloud Services: When using AI food analysis, images are temporarily processed through secure API services (Anthropic Claude) and are not permanently stored.
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API: For AI-powered food recognition and nutritional analysis. Their privacy policy can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and in-app purchases.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. All data stored on your device is encrypted. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.
  • Opt-out of notifications.
  • Request information about how your data is used.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@mealmeter.app
